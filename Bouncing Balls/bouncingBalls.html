Instructions

In this bouncing ball game, your objective is to control an "evil" ball that's tasked with eating all the colorful balls on the screen. You'll have a total of 25 colorful balls to eat. To win the game, you'll need to strategize and control the evil ball efficiently to complete the task in the shortest time possible.

  • ArrowLeft is used to move left
  • ArrowRight is used to move right
  • ArrowUp is used to move up
  • ArrowDown is used to move Down
